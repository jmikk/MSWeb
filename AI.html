Substitute Teacher Dashboard

AI High School

Supervised

Learning with a teacher. Uses labeled data to make predictions.

Example: Spam Filters

Unsupervised

Learning by patterns. Finds hidden clusters in unlabeled data.

Example: Customer Groups

Reinforcement

Learning by trial and error. Gets rewards or penalties for actions.

Example: Game-playing Bots
